Brooklyn, NY

I was born and raised in Brooklyn, NY.

I grew up in the vibrant neighborhood of Crown Heights. It's a lively community filled with Caribbean descendants, delicious food, vibrant music, captivating murals, and a friendly atmosphere.

Growing up in Brooklyn inspired me to learn how to play the bass guitar and alto saxophone. The neighborhood was always filled with the soulful sounds of jazz, and I couldn't help but be drawn to it.

Hiking

Hiking is one of my favorite pastimes.

My most memorable achievement so far has been conquering Yosemite National Park's Four Mile Trail. It's a challenging trail with an elevation gain of around 3,200 ft.

During my final year at Cornell, I had the opportunity to explore the beautiful trails, lakes, and waterfalls in Ithaca. Some of my favorites were Buttermilk Falls, Cascadilla Falls, and the Lake Treman Loop.

Playing Pool

Playing pool has become a beloved hobby of mine.

Initially, I wasn't interested in the game, perhaps because no one taught me how to properly play. But once I learned the hand positions for aiming and mastered controlling the power behind each shot, I quickly grew to enjoy it.

Now I'm learning new trick shots and ball spin tricks to add more excitement and competition when playing with friends and family.

Traveling

Traveling is a source of joy for me.

My family and I used to travel to Haiti every summer until 2008. After a 14-year hiatus, I finally had the chance to travel again with my sister for a much-needed end-of-the-school-year reset in Montego Bay, Jamaica. This trip allowed me to experience the beauty of other countries like Saint Thomas, Colombia, and Mexico, and I look forward to exploring more destinations in the future.

My goal is to visit 30 countries before turning 30, and I hope to embark on solo adventures for a third of those trips. With time, I aim to get closer to achieving that goal and broaden my horizons even further.

I’m available for full-time roles starting July 2023!

I’m always open to speak more about my experiences, previous and ongoing projects, and new software engineering opportunities.
